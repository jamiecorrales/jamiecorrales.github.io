About me!

Who is Jamie Corrales!

January-01-11 Jamie Corrales

My name is Jamie Corrales, and I am currently working in the post-secondary education sector as an instructor and academic coordinator. I teach various courses, but my primary specialty is economics and professional development. I like to learn how to code in my spare time, and I am currently taking the Data Science in Python certificate program at Datacamp. I'm the President of the Bay Street Breakfast Toastmasters Club in downtown Toronto where I'm currently helping the club achieve the Distinguished Toastmasters Club status (the highest status a club can achieve). Outside of all this, I manage an outdoor fitness club called Body Improvement Club, where I offer free boot camps at Riverdale Park East. I'm also an avid marathoner where I'm currently training for the Toronto Marathon in May and the Scotiabank Marathon in October - my goal is to qualify for the Bostom Marathon. If you like to learn more about what I do, feel free to reach out at corrales.jamie@gmail.com, and we can chat over Zoom or in-person at a local cafe!
