Intro

Hello, I appreciate you taking the time to check out my website. My name is Jamie Corrales, and I was born and raised in Toronto, Canada - best city in the world! Ever since I was a kid, I always had big aspirations and I still do. When I was a kid I wanted to become an artist, in highschool I wanted to become a army soldier, in university I wanted to be a wallstreet banker and now, I want to be an economist! I'm currently working on projects to help me achieve my career as an economist!

Work

I'm working towards a career as an Economist hopefully at the Bank of Canada. To help me get there, I've been furthering my knowledge in statistical analysis, econometrics and data science in R and Python. I don't have work experience so I'm currently working on an independent project, so stay tuned for that!

On the side, I've been learning how to create dashboards on Tableau where I've completed two projects to date. One of which is an interactive resume and a COVID-19 dashboard .

About

If you made it this far, you must really be interested in me! Well, I appreciate and grateful for it. To keep it short, I have my master's in economics and I currently teach business courses at a college and economics at a private high school.

Other than pursuing a career as an economist, I'm also part of Toastmasters - an organization that helps its members become better public speakers, so if you are interested in bettering the aformentioned skills, let's connect! I'm not done there! I also cofounded Bodyimprovementclub, a fitness club located in Toronto, Ontario! Check us out on Instagram @bodyimprovementclubto
